# Market pricing limitations

This repository produces **model-estimated fair probabilities** and
**zero-margin fair odds**:

\[
\text{Fair decimal odds} = \frac{1}{p}.
\]

That conversion does **not** include overround, market consensus, liability,
limits, injuries, news, or trader adjustments.

## Unsupported claims

This dataset cannot establish:

- market-beating information;
- closing-line value;
- expected return;
- tradable edge;
- profitable staking;
- commercially useful sportsbook pricing.

## What would be required

- time-stamped pregame bookmaker odds;
- de-vigged market probabilities;
- comparison against closing prices;
- log-loss improvement over the market;
- calibration relative to the market;
- closing-line value;
- simulated returns after vig and limits.

Until those exist, language is restricted to probability forecasting prototype
terminology.
